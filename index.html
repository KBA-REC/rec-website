What is KBA?

Kerala Blockchain Academy (KBA) is a Centre of Excellence under the Kerala University of Digital Sciences, Innovation and Technology (Digital University Kerala) focused on blockchain education, research and industry collaboration.

What is KBAIC?

KBA Innovation Club (KBAIC) is an initiative by KBA to help students explore blockchain & Web3 technologies through mentorship, hands-on projects and industry exposure.

What is KBA REC?

KBA REC is the Rajalakshmi Engineering College chapter of the KBAIC — a student-driven body that organises workshops, projects and industry-academia interactions under the guidance of KBA.

Center of Excellence: Kerala University of Digital Sciences, Innovation & Technology
Programs: Foundation to advanced blockchain developer courses, internships & startup programs
